# Social Sentry PC - Sales & Business Strategy

## 1. Value Proposition
**"Take Back Control of Your Digital Life"**
Social Sentry isn't just a blocker; it's a "Digital Bodyguard".
*   **USP (Unique Selling Point)**: "Zero Trust Privacy". Competitors track you in the cloud. Social Sentry keeps data 100% offline. Ideal for privacy-conscious parents and professionals.

## 2. Target Audiences

### A. The "Concerned Parent" (B2C)
*   **Pain Point**: Kids playing games during homework, visiting unsafe sites.
*   **Pitch**: "Protect your child without spying on them. Automated boundaries, offline security."
*   **Price Point**: $29 - $49 / Lifetime License (Single PC).

### B. The "Focus Seeker" / Freelancer (B2C)
*   **Pain Point**: Procrastination, Doom-scrolling.
*   **Pitch**: "Lock yourself out of distractions. Force productivity."
*   **Price Point**: $5/month or $40/year subscription.

### C. Small Business (B2B)
*   **Pain Point**: Employees wasting time on social media.
*   **Pitch**: "Non-invasive productivity tracking. Ensure work hours are actually for work."
*   **Price Point**: $10 / seat / month.

## 3. How to Sell (Channels)

### 1. Website (Landing Page)
*   Create a clean, high-converting landing page (Next.js/React).
*   **Sections**: "Features", "How it Works (Privacy)", "Testimonials", "Buy Now".
*   **Payment Processor**: Stripe or LemonSqueezy (Handles global taxes easily).

### 2. SEO & Content Marketing
*   Write blog posts: "How to block TikTok on Windows", "Best Parental Control for Privacy".
*   These bring free traffic from Google.

### 3. Marketplaces
*   **AppSumo**: List a "Lifetime Deal" to get initial users and feedback.
*   **Product Hunt**: Launch here for tech-savvy early adopters.

## 4. Monetization Model Recommendation
**"Freemium" Model**:
*   **Free Version**: Activity Tracking + 1 Block Rule.
*   **Pro Version ($49 Lifetime)**: Unlimited Blocking, Schedule, Advanced Analytics, Password Protection.

## 5. Risk Management
*   **Legal**: Ensure you have a clear Privacy Policy. State clearly that **NO DATA leaves the PC**.
*   **Support**: You need a support email. Parents will ask "Why is Fortnite still working?".
